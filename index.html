The Beagle Blog The Beagle is a breed of small scent hound, similar in appearance to the much larger foxhound. The Beagle was developed primarily for hunting hare, known as beagling. Possessing a great sense of smell and superior tracking instincts, the Beagle is the primary breed used as a detection dog for prohibited agricultural imports and foodstuffs in quarantine around the world.
